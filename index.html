  • Overview
  • Our Philosophy

An award-winning nonprofit childcare center located in Chapel Hill, NC.

  • Full Time Care

    We are open year-round, Monday through Friday, from 7:30am to 5:30pm (except for designated holidays and teacher workdays).

  • Parent Involvement and Management

    As a member-run center, we believe a child's preschool experience is enhanced when parents and teachers work together as partners. A parent-led board works with the director to administer the center’s operations.

  • Learning Through Play

    Our curriculum is designed to help children develop emotional, physical, and cognitive skills. Play is critical for building foundational experiences with taking risks and navigating social interactions.

  • Access for All

    Our mission is to provide all children with the high quality and affordable care that they and their parents deserve.

  • Respect

    We believe each child is unique and deserves respect and attention. We value each child's cultural heritage and respect the differences among us.

Our Classes

Age appropriate education in a loving, stimulating, and secure environment.

Our five classrooms serve up to 47 children, ranging in age from 6 weeks to 6 years old.

Small class size coupled with dedicated teachers is our model. With a high teacher to student ratio, and low staff and family turnover rates, each child is surrounded by caregivers who have watched them grow—and who know and treat them as an indiviudal.
